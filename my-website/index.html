Why POILY

The Problem

Chronic diseases are responsible for 7 out of 10 deaths each year in the US, amounting to 1.6 million people, and nearly 90% of the nation's healthcare costs, totaling 3 trillion dollars.

The Opportunity

The good news is that nearly 80% of chronic diseases can be prevented through simple lifestyle changes like reducing drug use, limiting alcohol consumption, managing stress, incorporating regular exercise, and making healthier food choices.

The Solution

From governments to grocers, organizations are investing resources and ideas into lifestyle health opportunities to combat the chronic disease crisis in America. Unfortunately, our nation's investment in lifestyle health initiatives does not match the size of the opportunity. However, it is crucial to tell the stories of those on the front lines delivering lifestyle health solutions in order to shift the nation's mindset and investment priorities. At Poily Analytics, we partner with organizations to visualize their lifestyle health program stories so that they are heard loud and clear.

Who we Serve

Government

Government combats chronic disease through public policy and funding health initiatives. Telling the story of resources and funding committed to lifestyle health initiatives is crucial in gaining public awareness and support in the prevention and management of chronic diseases.

Insurers

Insurers combat chronic diseases by incentivizing healthy lifestyle choices and providing coverage for preventative care services. Data stories provide insights into the impact of lifestyle health initiatives on cost and outcomes. This can drive alignment with providers and engagement with members.

Employers

Employers combat chronic diseases by promoting lifestyle health within the organizational culture through partner programs and employee incentives. Telling the story of derived organizational and personal value can help maximize impact potential.

Non-profits:

Non-profits combat chronic diseases by raising awareness, promoting healthy lifestyles, and providing resources and support to individuals affected by chronic diseases. Impact stories need to be told in order to secure ongoing funding and engagement from the communities they serve.

Food Retailers

Food retailers fight against chronic disease by providing consumers with access to and promoting healthy food options. Data stories can reach and communicate to consumers the value proposition of the retailer’s lifestyle health efforts.

Health Systems

Health Systems fight against chronic disease by embedding lifestyle health solutions alongside clinical pathways. Clearly communicating lifestyle health impact on outcomes and cost garners the ongoing support of payers and the engagement of patients.
